Project Umang


Integrating Career and Academic Advising

The young minds of Pilani believe in shaping and nurturing more young minds by mentoring students personally throughout the year. We also provide scholarships to poor and meritorious students and arrange career counselling sessions. We are Umang, and we bring change in Pilani through Our hard work and Education

WORKING:
  • We provide scholarships to poor children with good academics by paying a fraction of their school fees.The actual economic condition of the student is decided by doing home visits, while to check his academics, we consider his last yearâ€™s performance as well as we have our own tests to decide whether the student is deserving or not. Taking all these factors into consideration we have a specially designed scholarship formula, so that every applicant gets the amount of scholarship he deserves eliminating partiality and human errors of any sort.
  • We conduct planned set of counselling sessions throughout the year incorporating various topics ranging from Motivational Sessions, How to Study, to soft issues like Women Health and important issues like Career Counselling.
  • We have the Specific Mentorship Program. We all know that one-day motivational sessions are not enough and we need continuous guidance, so we came up with this program in which each student is allotted a volunteer which takes care of his personal as well as academic issues throughout the year.
  • We coach students for competitive exams such as IIT-JEE and AIPMT. We have a special team of Umang Volunteers who coach about 15 students for JEE and AIPMT.
