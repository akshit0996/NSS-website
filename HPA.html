Health and Public Awareness


Spreading Awareness in Society

Health and public awareness, a joint department of Health and Public Awareness, is meant for spreading awareness among people on various social problems and aware them about potential solution of these problems .Also, to aware them about ways to enhance their livings through proper use of technology and intellect. Helping people by making Government policies of mass welfare within their reach by serving as a link between people and government.

WORKING:
  • It has worked on a few projects like water management with rainwater harvesting, water filtration, improving education facilities in Rayla school, smooth functioning of sanctioned medical vans, workshops on skill development for women.
  • Currently Health and Public Awareness team is working on Vocational Training Center(VTC) Project, waste management in Jherli, awareness about the implementation of organic farming as a sustainable way of farming, resolving the electricity problem in Rayla.
  • The main villages where Health and Public Awareness team focuses are:Rayla,Garinda,Jherli,Harinagar,Pilani rural
  • Apart from all the above sectors, Health and Public Awareness team also works in a few other villages on occasional basis.
