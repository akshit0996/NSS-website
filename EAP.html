Events and Publicity


Events for the Needy

Team events and publicity is the department which organises various events to uplift specific social cause and also undertake various publicity work to publicise the work of NSS in whole.

WORKING:
  • JUNOON: Through junoon, we aim at spreading joy among the specially-abled community. We work tirelessly to ensure that they have memorable and exciting 2 days experience. Various cultural, sporting and recreational activities are organised for them to uplift their spirit. This event fills everyone with immense “junoon”.
  • SHOP FOR A SMILE: The motto of oasis stall is to promote various ngos which work tirelessly for the betterment of the society. We sell their products and provide them a platform to present their work. We also organise secondary events to create awareness and uplift various social cause.
  • BLOOD DONATION CAMP: There is no need to explain the importance of blood donation. NSS organises blood donation camp within campus wherein all the students as well as pilani residents come and donate life saver “blood”.
  • APOGEE EVENT: In the technical fest of the campus, we organise technical presentations wherein various other social groups come and present their work. Our aim through this event is to learn from others through mutual collaboration.
