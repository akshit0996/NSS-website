Umang Collection

Umang Collection is basically a crowd funding arranged by NSS, BITS Pilani to sponsor the education of poor and meritorious students in Pilani.
The main aim of this entire collection is EDUCATION FOR ALL

Junoon' 17

Junoon is a two day sporting extravaganza held annually for the specially abled by NSS BITS Pilani. It witnesses a participation of over 200 students from various NGOs spread across the country.

SmartApp Camp

A techno camp was organised to spread awareness about the working processes of some of the popular mobile apps among the people of Pilani and its nearby villages.
